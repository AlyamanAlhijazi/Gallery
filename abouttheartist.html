About The Artist

Meet the Artist: Aisha Alhijazi

Since 2017, Aisha has called the Netherlands her home, but her artistic journey began long before her arrival on Dutch soil. Born in Syria in 1998, she embarked on her creative path at a tender age, initially exploring the world of drawing and later transitioning to the captivating realm of oil painting in 2018. It was a turning point that kindled a lifelong passion for capturing emotions and beauty on canvas.

A Passion for Expressive Realism

Aisha's artistic style is a mesmerizing fusion of expressiveness and realism. Her oil paintings delve deep into the human experience, with a particular focus on capturing the subtlest emotions and intricate details in portraits and still life. Through the vibrant medium of oil paint, she conjures a rich palette of colors, infusing her work with an enchanting depth that emphasizes texture and vitality in her subjects.

Eyes: The Gateway to the Soul

When painting portraits, Aisha places extraordinary emphasis on the eyes. She firmly believes that the eyes are windows to the soul, powerful conduits of emotion. Through meticulous attention to anatomy, light reflection, and the nuances of the eyes, she invites viewers to explore the inner worlds of her subjects. Her goal is for you to read the feelings, thoughts, and experiences etched in the eyes, forging a profound emotional connection with each piece.

Capturing the Essence of Still Life

In still life compositions, Aisha discovers boundless inspiration. Here, she celebrates the beauty and transience of everyday objects. Playing with light and shadow, she weaves a tapestry of drama and ambiance, while her meticulous attention to texture and materials breathes life into the ordinary. Through her brush, she immortalizes these moments in oil, bestowing timelessness and significance upon the seemingly mundane.

Experience Art That Speaks to the Heart

Aisha's journey from Syria to the Netherlands and her evolution as an artist have culminated in a captivating body of work. Each stroke of her brush tells a story, inviting you to join her in exploring the depths of emotion, the beauty in the everyday, and the timeless connection between artist and viewer. Discover the world through her eyes and her art.
